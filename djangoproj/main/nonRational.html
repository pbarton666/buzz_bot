These are just vignettes, but we're clearly not in a world dominated by the rational or the fully-informed. Given this, what can we do to understand and engage people around important topics like green energy and conservation? PES takes this approach:
Understand the consumers' relationship with the utility sector actors:
- Interaction area between customers and client
- Indirect factors (company image or industry reputation, say)
- Social factors (e.g., peer group approval)
- Barriers (real or perceived) to engagement
Design/execute a survey instrument to discover:
- Overall attitudes regarding each key component of the relationship
- Attitudes around specific characteristics (attributes) of each component
- Specific barriers to initiating/sustaining the relationship
- Likelihood to engage/persist in specific behaviors related to the relationship
Develop and fit a causal, predictive model which estimates:
- Strenghts of association between relationship elements and behaviors
- Strengths of association among different relationship elements
- Finds "critical path" links between potential strategies and customer behavior
...all of which can inform a behavior based market segmentation
