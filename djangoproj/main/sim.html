Our modeling team builds customized, interactive simulation models, and has done so for major energy industry clients including Bonneville, Sandia National Laboratory, Salt River Project, and U.S. DOE.

We create network and stand-alone "Nintendo games for adults" that allow users to learn by interacting with virtual systems.
As internal management tools, simulators are a proven way to:
facilitate learner-driven understanding
communicate research findings
quickly screen disparate investment initiatives based on likely ROI, NPV or other criteria
As market communication tools, these tools provide a means to:
engage customers in a fact-based dialog
educate customers on alternatives and their implications
provide feedback to reinforce the benefits of their decisions
PES specializes in dynamic simualtion modeling, but also has capability to conduct Bayesian network analysis and develop agent-based AI systems and genetic probramming applications.
