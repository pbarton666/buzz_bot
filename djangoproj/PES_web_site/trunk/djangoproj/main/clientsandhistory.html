PES was originally formed in 1991 and operated successfully until 1996, serving clients in a variety of scientific, corporate, and government organizations.  Shortly thereafter the founder, Pat Barton joined forces with Dr. Dianne Marozas (now Dianne Barton) a fellow energy scientist.  Pat and Dianne assembled a team of social and hard scientists and re-launched PES in 2009 to support the green energy industry.
